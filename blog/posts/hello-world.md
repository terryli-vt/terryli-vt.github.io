This is my first post.

I've wanted to write more consistently for a while, and having a dedicated space helps.

## What I'll write about

Mostly things I'm figuring out in public:

- Engineering decisions I found interesting
- Design details worth paying attention to
- Tools, patterns, and ideas I keep returning to
- Occasionally, things completely unrelated to code

## How this blog works

Posts are plain Markdown files. No CMS, no database — just text files rendered in the browser with [marked.js](https://marked.js.org/).

It's the simplest possible setup. That's intentional.
